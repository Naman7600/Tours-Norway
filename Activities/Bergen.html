Explore Bergen: Popular Activities

Discover the best of Norway's scenic city by the fjords

Bryggen

Explore the historic wooden houses of Bergen’s iconic Bryggen wharf, a UNESCO site.

Mount Fløyen

Take the Fløibanen funicular for stunning views over Bergen from the top of Mount Fløyen.

Trolltunga

Challenge yourself with a hike to Trolltunga, one of Norway's most famous rock formations.
